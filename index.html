Mission

Our mission is both saving and changing the lifestyle of the orphans in Haiti. When a child loses his parents and becomes homeless, H.O.L does whatever it takes to make sure that child has access to education, to food, to health and to be part of family--Just as we would do if a child in our family were become homeless.

What We Do

The goal of HOL is to remove as many children as possible from the many thousands of children abandoned in the streets and have nowhere to go.
